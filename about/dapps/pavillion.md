# Pavillion

Intuitive hub bridging the gap between mainstream gaming and blockchain. Pavillion is a one stop shop for hosting and connecting chain-enabled games to private wallets via Phantasma. Pavillion makes it easy for users to buy, browse, trade and access on-chain assets for hosted titles. It also connects on-chain assets to Steam.
